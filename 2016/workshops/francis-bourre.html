hexMachina workshop

In this workshop we will target Flash and JS platforms and we will highlight: Low-coupling, reusability, and last but not least collaborative work.

These are the golden principles of hexMachina framework. Depending of the number of attendees, we will set the topic (of the demo app) and team members tasks to reach the final goal.
