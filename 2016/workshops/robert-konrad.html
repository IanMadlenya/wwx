Kha workshop

In this workshop we will help you to get started with Kha, the super portable software dev kit and its accompanying IDE Kode Studio. Kha's programming workflow and its capabilities for 2D and 3D graphics are introduced, including some shader and SIMD programming.

by Robert Konrad

Robert does development work for serious games, less serious games and gaming related research projects. He is best known for creating Kha, a Haxe based cutting edge multimedia framework.
